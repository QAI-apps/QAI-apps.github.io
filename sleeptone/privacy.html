SleepTone Pro - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Overview

SleepTone Pro ("the App") is a mobile soundscape app for sleep, focus, and relaxation, developed by QAI ("we", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

The App is designed to work entirely offline. Your sound presets, favorites, and settings are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

We use the following third-party services that may collect limited data:

  • Google AdMob - Displays banner advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics - Collects anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which features are used, app crashes) to help us improve the App. No personal information is collected. Firebase Privacy
  • RevenueCat - Manages subscription purchases. RevenueCat processes transaction data provided by Apple/Google. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Advertising

The free version of the App displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. No interstitial or full-screen ads are shown. You can remove all advertisements by subscribing to SleepTone Pro Premium. On iOS, the App requests your permission via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before allowing ad-related tracking.

Subscriptions

SleepTone Pro offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions managed through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Payment is processed by Apple/Google, not by us. We do not have access to your payment information.

Audio & Microphone

The App plays audio files bundled within the App. It does not access your microphone or record any audio.

Data Storage

All your data (presets, favorites, recently played sounds, settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any backend servers. If you delete the App, your data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Since your data is stored only on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete all data by uninstalling the App.

For GDPR (EU) users: You may request information about data processing by contacting us. The App shows a consent banner on first launch for EU users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
